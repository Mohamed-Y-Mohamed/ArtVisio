Instructions

Getting around.



To navigate through the virtual gallery, you will need to use the WASD keys for movement, where each key corresponds to a specific direction: 'W' to move forward, 'A' to move left, 'S' to move backward, and 'D' to move right. Additionally, controlling the direction of your movement and your point of view within this immersive environment requires the use of a mouse. By moving the mouse, you can adjust your perspective and the direction you are facing, allowing for a more intuitive and engaging exploration of the virtual space. This combination of keyboard and mouse controls ensures a seamless and interactive experience as you explore the artworks displayed in the virtual gallery.

View Artist and artwork details:



To delve deeper into the details of the artist and their artwork, simply approach the piece that captures your interest within the virtual gallery. As you get closer to the specific artwork, a pop-up will automatically appear, presenting you with valuable information about both the artist and the artwork itself. Should you wish to explore further, clicking on the artwork will navigate you to a dedicated page showcasing the artist's biography, including their social media links and any additional information they have chosen to share. This feature enriches your experience by providing an in-depth look at the creative minds behind the art, all at the click of a button and the convenience of your approach.
