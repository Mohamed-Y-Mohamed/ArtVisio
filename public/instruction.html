Instruction

Navigating the Gallery in VR:

Dive into our virtual gallery using VR to experience art like never before. Use the WASD keys for directional movement: 'W' to advance forward, 'A' for left, 'S' to step back, and 'D' to move right. Control your viewpoint with the mouse, moving it to look around. This immersive control scheme brings the virtual gallery to life, allowing you to explore artworks in a dynamic and engaging manner.

Immerse Yourself in the Artistic Experience

Our use of VR and Three.js technology offers a revolutionary way to interact with art, providing depth and a sense of presence that traditional viewing methods cannot match. Step into the future of art exploration with us.

Featured Artwork: The Annunciation by Fra Angelico

Experience historical masterpieces like "The Annunciation" in a new light, making the rich history of art accessible and engaging for all.

Exploring the Gallery on PC:

PC users can navigate the gallery with a game-like experience. Use the WASD or arrow keys for movement and the mouse to change your viewing angle. This intuitive setup allows for effortless exploration of the gallery, making art more accessible and enjoyable from your desktop.

Bringing Art Closer to You

Our platform merges technology and art, offering an enriched experience that highlights the beauty and history of artworks from around the world.
